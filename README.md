# Quiz Game
A Pokémon guessing quiz built with Godot powered by the PokéAPI inspired by the show



##  About

A 10 round "Who's That Pokémon?" quiz game Each round shows a silhouetted Pokémon sprite and gives you 4 name choices Guess correctly to score points
the answer is revealed after each round whether you're right or wrong

##  Features

- **Live PokéAPI integration**  fetches all 898 Pokémon names and sprites at runtime
- **Silhouette reveal**  Pokémon sprite is blacked out during the question then revealed in full colour after answering
- **Colour feedback**  correct answer highlights green wrong answer highlights red
- **Score tracking**  tracks your score across all 10 rounds
- **finasl score screen**  shows your final score out of 10 when the quiz ends

## How to Play

1. A silhouetted Pokémon appears on screen
2. Choose one of the 4 name options
3. The Pokémon is revealed and your answer is highlighted and reveals the pokemon
5. After 2 seconds the next round begins
6. After 10 rounds your final score is shown
